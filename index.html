ALLE(le)S

By Kenza Samlali

About

Alle(le)s is an interactive web app that allows you
to experience the simplicity and raw form of life: nucleotides.
All of life's genetic information consists in its purest form
of a string of nucleotides (A, C, T, G bases).
A huge database called NCBI contains sequences of millions of living organisms.
Use this WebApp to search it,
and to draw out a string of nucleotides to experience the length of these sequences

Instructions:


Search the nucleotide database for an animal, tissue, cell line, gene...
Example: mus musculus
Drag to draw DNA
Drag Two fingers to make RNA
